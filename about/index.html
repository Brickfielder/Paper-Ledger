"Brickfieder" is a nod to my long-standing passion for meteorology. A Brickfielder is a hot, dry, and dusty northwesterly wind that blows across southern Australia from the interior deserts during late spring and summer. I have always liked complex systems, and tried to understand them as much as possible.
